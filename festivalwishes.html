Best day of the week - Friday

📖 Surah Al-Kahf Ki Tilawat

Jumma ke din Surah Al-Kahf parhna sunnat hai

" مَنْ قَرَأَ سُورَةَ الْكَهْفِ يَوْمَ الْجُمُعَةِ أَضَاءَ لَهُ مِنَ النُّورِ مَا بَيْنَ الْجُمُعَتَيْنِ "
"Jo Shakhs Jumma ke din Surah Al-Kahf parhta hai, do Jumma ke darmiyan uske liye noor hota hai"

⭐ Jumma Ke Sunnah Actions:

  • 🛁 Ghusl karna (Take bath)
  • 👔 Acha kapra pehenna (Wear best clothes)
  • 👃 Itr lagana (Apply perfume)
  • 🚶 Paidal masjid jana (Walk to mosque if possible)
  • 🕯️ Darood shareef parhna (Send blessings on Prophet PBUH)
  • 🤲 Dua qabool hone ka waqt (Last hour of Friday)
